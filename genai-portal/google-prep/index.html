Focused preparation roadmap

Complete phases in order. Later topics unlock only when the previous readiness gate is met.

01

Python fluency reset

Write, test and debug ordinary programs without AI.

Core syntax, functions, collections and comprehensions
Classes, dataclasses, exceptions and modules
Iterators, generators and context managers
pytest, mocking, logging and debugging
Build a CLI data-processing tool from scratch
Build and test a FastAPI CRUD service
Gate: complete a 90-minute coding session with AI disabled; write a small API plus tests and explain every design choice.
02

DSA foundations

Learn patterns, not a pile of remembered solutions.

Arrays, strings, hash maps and sets
Two pointers, sliding window, prefix sums and binary search
Stacks, queues and linked lists
Trees, BST, heaps, BFS and DFS
Graphs, topological sort and union-find
Backtracking, greedy and dynamic programming
Solve and review 100 pattern-balanced problems
Gate: solve an unseen medium problem in 35 minutes, communicate clearly, test edge cases and state time/space complexity.
03

Production Python engineering

Move from scripts to maintainable services.

HTTP, REST, validation, auth, pagination and idempotency
PostgreSQL schemas, indexes, transactions and query plans
Processes, threads, asyncio and concurrency trade-offs
Type checking, linting, unit/integration tests and CI
Linux, networking, Docker, profiling and observability
Ship one production-grade backend project
Gate: defend architecture, schema, failure handling, test strategy, performance and deployment of your own service.
04

System design

Build trade-off reasoning from components upward.

Latency, throughput, capacity estimation and bottlenecks
SQL/NoSQL, replication, partitioning and consistency
Load balancers, caches, queues, streams and workers
Retries, idempotency, fault tolerance and observability
Complete 12 timed designs from small to large
Design a Python job platform and a data/ML platform
Gate: lead a 45-minute design conversation: clarify, estimate, propose, find bottlenecks, evolve and discuss trade-offs.
05

Google interview execution

Convert knowledge into consistent interview performance.

Rewrite and defend every resume bullet
Prepare 12 STAR stories with evidence and reflection
Leadership, conflict, ambiguity and prioritization scenarios
Complete 20 timed coding mocks
Complete 10 system-design mocks
Pass three full mock loops without a major weak round
Gate: no repeated critical feedback across three consecutive mock loops; explanations remain structured under pressure.

General backend and platform engineering. Your portfolio must prove APIs, databases, concurrency, reliability, testing and clean code without depending on ML.

Secondary: ML/data systems SDE

Model serving, data pipelines, feature systems, batch/stream processing and evaluation infrastructure—where your domain history strengthens the story.

Level policy

Rules that keep preparation honest

✓
AI may coach; it may not perform the repetition for you.

Attempt first, request hints second, reimplement from memory, and regularly practise in an AI-free editor. Do not treat familiarity as mastery; reproduce the work independently.
