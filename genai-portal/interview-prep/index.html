A better practice loop

1

Answer closed-book

Give yourself 60–90 seconds before opening the answer. Start with the principle, then a production example.

2

Add a trade-off

Say what you would choose, why, and what constraint would make you choose differently.

3

Design aloud

Move to the Scenario Studio and practise architecture, scale, security and evaluation as one connected answer.
